# Product Mission (Lite)

GOODMAN GLS is a B2B logistics company website and partner portal that helps global freight forwarders, Korean exporters/importers, and airline cargo teams access Korea's logistics market by providing streamlined rate inquiries, shipment management, and GSA/CSA airline partnerships through a single digital platform.

GOODMAN GLS serves international logistics professionals who need reliable Korean market access for air, ocean, and project cargo. As a Korean forwarder with dual network membership (MPL + EAN), the platform combines deep local expertise with a digital-first approach, delivering 24-hour rate responses and self-service partner tools that eliminate traditional email/phone bottlenecks.
