# Product Mission (Lite)

GOODMAN GLS is a B2B logistics company website and partner portal operated by a leading Korean GSSA (General Sales Agent / Cargo Sales Agent). It helps global logistics partners, airlines, and cargo teams access Korea's air cargo market by providing streamlined rate inquiries, capacity management, and airline cargo sales representation through a single digital platform.

GOODMAN GLS serves international logistics professionals who need reliable Korean market access for air cargo and supplementary ocean/project cargo services. As Korea's premier GSSA with dual network membership (MPL + EAN), the platform combines deep airline partnerships with a digital-first approach, delivering 24-hour rate responses and self-service partner tools that eliminate traditional email/phone bottlenecks.
